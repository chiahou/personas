Goals: What do they want to achieve with this app

According to the sellers we've interviewed, we can concluded that there are two key factors contributing to their business success:

  • Price
  • Rating

How to improve rating?
According to our sellers, the more resposive your are, the higher rate you can get.

Solutions: What should we provide within this app

Mobile apps can provide immediacy, helping our sellers to act more responsively in two ways:

  1. Reply to customers' question quickly
  2. Manage customers' orders in a timely fashion
