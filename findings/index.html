TA: Who wants a seller app and why

Those in need of seller apps are often rather small in company size, medium in business size on Rakuten TW ichiba, and self-managed in terms of team type. The company size is usually not big enough, less than 20 people, to have functional structures. They ususally have one employee do multiple jobs and it is often the founder/CEO who does the most. Whoever the Rakuten shop manager is, they treat their job like their own child.

Goals: What do they want to achieve with this app

According to the sellers we've interviewed, we can concluded that there are two key factors contributing to their business success:

  • Price
  • Rating

How to improve rating?
According to our sellers, the more resposive your are, the higher rate you can get.

As mobile devices allow sellers to work wherever and whenever they need to, they can respond to customers quickly.

Solutions: What should we provide within this app

Mobile apps can provide immediacy, helping our sellers to act more responsively in two ways:

  1. Reply to customers' question quickly
    • FAQ: Do you have this item in stock?
    • FAQ: When will my order be dispatched? How long does it take?
  2. Manage customers' orders in a timely fashion
    • Be informed when new orders are placed
    • Check out order status
    • Update product listing and pricing
